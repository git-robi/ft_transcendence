# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 31, 2026**

## 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how our educational Pong game platform (a 42 School project) collects, uses, and protects your information.

## 2. Information We Collect
We collect:
- **Account Information**: Username, email address, and authentication credentials
- **Game Data**: Game statistics, match history, and gameplay information
- **OAuth Data**: If you sign in with GitHub or Google, we receive basic profile information

## 3. How We Use Your Information
Your information is used to:
- Provide and maintain the game service
- Track game statistics and match history
- Enable authentication and account management
- Improve the user experience

## 4. Data Storage
- All data is stored securely in our PostgreSQL database
- We implement industry-standard security measures
- This is an educational project - data retention is limited to the project duration

## 5. Data Sharing
**We do not sell or share your data with third parties.** This is a non-commercial educational project.

## 6. Third-Party Services
We use OAuth authentication with:
- **GitHub**: For authentication purposes
- **Google**: For authentication purposes

These services have their own privacy policies.

## 7. Cookies
We use essential cookies for:
- Authentication and session management
- Maintaining your LogIn state

## 8. Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Request deletion of your account and data
- Opt out of data collection (by not using the service)

## 9. Children's Privacy
This service is designed for users 13 years and older.

## 10. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of changes.

## 11. Contact
For privacy concerns or data requests, contact the project administrators.

## 12. Educational Project Notice
This is a student project created for learning purposes at 42 School. It is not intended for commercial use or long-term data storage.
